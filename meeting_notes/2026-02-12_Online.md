 ## Topics discussed

- Funding approved for RSE & Women in HPC meeting, along with RSE Midlands 2026 travel bursaries
- Conference sponsorship are waiting for results of final vote to approach any sponsors in the proposed areas of exclusion
- Direct debit issues with membership platform are gradually being resolved. Long term is to move direct debit to stripe.
- RSECon26 finances seem are in a reasonable state. Based on previous conference, our estimated scenarios:
    - Min: Deficit of £10,000
    - Likely: Surplus of £9,000
    - Max: Surplus of £37,000
- 26 Mentor-mentee pairs from the mentoring programme, had 5 more mentees than available mentors
- We will run am online SocRSE feedback event to get input from the community on what they want to discuss
- Suggestion of creating RSE Fellowships (similar to SSI Fellowships), administered by OLS as a pilot programme
- Policy and Funding working group will need to recruit members - discussion around the best way forward wasn't resolved
- Next meeting will be in person, where we will aim to discuss:
    - Spending down of our surplus funds 
    - Do we want to collect EDIA data in our membership platform to be able to assess our diversity
    - Summary report of members survey

 ## Topics discussed

- Finance has started to address issues with renewals not being processed, currently requires some manual updating of members updates but hoping to clear the backlog of about 70 members. 
- RSECon26 meetings are now kicking off
  - No concerns from trustees to establish a JOSS Tools Track
  - Strong negative feedback from trustees for naming it RSEConX as the 10th conference
- RSECon27 call for programme chair(s) in January 2026
- We are aiming to run a feedback event in February. We'll also use this to share our CoARA action plan and the results of the membership vote on exclusion criteria for ethical sponsorship policy.
- Global Talent Visas for RSEs
  - Changes to visa requirements/settlement status will lead to people being unable to apply for settled status.
  - We will speak to RSE leaders about their experiences and form a response
